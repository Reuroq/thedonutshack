Our story

Hi, we're The Donut Shack

The Donut Shack started where a lot of good things do — a tiny home kitchen, a stand mixer that wouldn't quit, and Daisy's stubborn belief that a donut could be both ridiculously pretty and ridiculously good.

After years of bringing boxes to every birthday, baby shower, and bridal brunch in the valley, the asks kept coming: 'Can you do my wedding?' 'Can you bring these to the office?' So Daisy did the only sensible thing — she put the whole operation on wheels and painted it pink.

Today the Shack rolls all over Las Vegas, hand-glazing small-batch donuts to order with real ingredients, real flowers, and a whole lot of joy. Every event we pull up to feels a little more like a celebration — and that's exactly the point.
