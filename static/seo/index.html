Our story

One little pink truck, a whole lot of joy

The Donut Shack started where a lot of good things do — a tiny home kitchen, a stand mixer that wouldn't quit, and Daisy's stubborn belief that a donut could be both ridiculously pretty and ridiculously good.

It grew into a pink truck that rolls all over the valley, hand-glazing small-batch donuts to order. Pretty, playful, and made with real ingredients.
